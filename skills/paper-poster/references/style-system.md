# Poster Style System

## Presets

### conference-wide

目标：标准学术会议展板，信息完整、层级清晰、专业感强。

- Background: white / near-white, subtle gradient bottom
- Color: academic blue (#2563eb) + charcoal (#1a1a1a) + light gray
- Typography: bold sans-serif titles, serif/clean sans body
- Visual: 1 main method figure (40%), 2-3 supporting panels
- Text: title 1 line, sections 3-6 bullet points each, references 4-6 items
- Feel: NeurIPS / ICML / CVPR standard conference poster
- Avoid: cartoon, heavy texture, fake 3D, neon colors, decorative blobs

### defense-poster

目标：答辩/毕业展板，结构清晰、评审友好、重点突出。

- Background: white, clean
- Color: dark blue (#1e3a5f) + white + warm accent (#f59e0b)
- Typography: large readable titles, clear body
- Visual: method figure center, results on right, conclusion bottom
- Text: moderate density — each section gets space to breathe
- Feel: clear academic argument, easy to follow standing 1m away
- Avoid: overcrowding, tiny text, low contrast

### research-showcase

目标：开放日/课题展示，视觉冲击力强、传播感好。

- Background: dark gradient (charcoal to deep blue)
- Color: white + cyan (#06b6d4) + amber (#f59e0b)
- Typography: very large bold titles, minimal body
- Visual: 1 hero image/illustration + 2-3 data callouts
- Text: minimal — titles only, details in handout
- Feel: premium research showcase, Apple keynote poster style
- Avoid: dense text, small labels, traditional academic layout
